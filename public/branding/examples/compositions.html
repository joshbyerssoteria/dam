ABOUT SOTERIA
A church
for the ordinary.
We’re a Baptist church in West Des Moines, Iowa — making more and better disciples, one Sunday at a time. The gospel is still good news for people who are tired, unsure, or just starting over.
Fourteen years later, we're still at it — preaching through whole books of the Bible, gathering in homes, and making room for whoever walks through the door.
THE PULPIT
Verse-by-verse, all the way through.
Expositional preaching is our oldest conviction.

Small, weekly, shared meals. Shared life.
