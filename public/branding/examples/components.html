COMPONENTS
Buttons
All buttons are pills — `border-radius: 9999px`. Primary fills with the brand color; on hover the fill drops to transparent, the border appears in the same color, the label slides 8px left, padding-right grows ~10px to make room, and an icon (heart-pin, door, arrow) slides in from the right. Same 500ms ease-out for every property. One primary per composition. Secondary is the outlined-at-rest version that inverts to a fill on hover. Tertiary stays as a gold-underlined text link.
